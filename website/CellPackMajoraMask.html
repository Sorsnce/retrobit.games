The Legends of Zelda: Majora's Mask CelPack for GlideN64

The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask CellPack is redesigned version of Majora's Mask. This texture pack includes new textures from the ground up with HD support. The theme of this texture pack is too mirror The Legends of Zelda: Wind Waker. Please see below on steps to setup this texture pack.


Information about the Original Game.

Release: October 26, 2000

Console: Nintendo 64

Developer: Nintendo EAD

Title: The Legend of Zelda: Majora's Mask

Source: Wikipedia



Information about the Mod.

Developer: Djipi

Release Date: 2011

Source: http://www.emutalk.net/threads/56437-2016-Majora-s-Mask-Cel-Pack-for-GlideN64

Status: Complete
