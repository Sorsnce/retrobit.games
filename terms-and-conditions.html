Terms and conditions

ORDER CONFIRMATION
Once an order is completed, the customer will automatically receive a confirmation of the purchase.

AGE RESTRICTION
If the customer is under 18, permission must be obtained from a parent or other person of authority.

PAYMENT
If payment is by debit or credit card, the customer must enter all the necessary information on the card and, in the case of a transaction with 3D Secure, the security code. Maaemo does not store customer credit card information after completing the transaction

LOCAL LEVIES
Please note that local import duties vary by country and region. Foreign taxes are to be paid by the customer.

RETURNS
Goods may be returned within 14 calendar days from date of receipt. The product must be returned in original packaging. Return costs are the responsibility of the customer.

SHIPPING
Maaemo sends packages with Norwegian Postal Service.

PRIVACY
Maaemo treats customer information with the utmost respect and integrity. No information will be disclosed to third parties.
