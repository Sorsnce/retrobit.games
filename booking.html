Make a table
reservation

Table reservations are made via our online table reservation system. If there aren't any tables available on the date you request, you can sign up for our waiting list.

We receive cancellations daily. Please note that we receive a lot of requests for reservations for two persons. It is more likely to secure a reservation for the desired date if the party size is more than two.

For all other matters regarding bookings, please send us a mail or call us
